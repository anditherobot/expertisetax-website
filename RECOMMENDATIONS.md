## Long-term (Technical & Marketing)

### 9. Google Business Profile
- **What**: Claim/optimize your Google Business listing
- **Why**: Show up in "near me" searches and Google Maps
- **Action Items**:
  - Claim listing at google.com/business
  - Add photos, hours, services
  - Request reviews from satisfied clients
- **Impact**: Local SEO visibility, more walk-ins/calls
- **Time**: ~2 hours initial setup + ongoing

### 10. Add Live Chat or Chatbot
- **What**: Install live chat widget (e.g., Tawk.to, Tidio, Intercom)
- **Why**: Answer simple questions instantly, capture leads 24/7
- **Impact**: Capture more leads, better user experience
- **Time**: ~1 hour to integrate

### 11. Blog for Content Marketing
- **What**: Create blog with tax-related content
- **Topics**:
  - Seasonal tax tips
  - Tax law changes
  - Local tax issues (Massachusetts-specific)
  - Multilingual content for your diverse client base
- **Why**: Drives organic traffic year-round, establishes expertise
- **Impact**: Long-term traffic growth, better SEO
- **Time**: Ongoing (1-2 posts per month)

### 12. Performance Optimization
- **What**: Technical improvements for faster loading
- **Actions**:
  - Compress images (services.jpg, profile.png, workwithpro.jpg)
  - Add lazy loading for images
  - Enable browser caching
  - Minify CSS/JS
  - Consider CDN for static assets
- **Why**: Faster site = better user experience + better SEO
- **Impact**: Improved page speed, better rankings
- **Time**: ~3 hours
